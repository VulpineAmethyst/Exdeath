Waddler Rebalance

Hello there! First of all, thank you for taking the time to read this or even consider playing my rebalance. I really appreciate it! You can feel free to give me critique or suggestions or report any bugs in the FFV Goons Discord server, or message me directly at The Great Waddler#3967 on Discord.

My design goals for this hack are pretty simple: Give the weaker or less interesting jobs more interesting or powerful things to do. In addition to that, I'll be doing my best to fix some old bugs in the game that have been in there forever, ergo the Power Drink not actually increasing your attack and !Mineuchi having the proper paralysis associated with it.

Later on, I'll be releasing a secondary hack alongside this one that fine tunes bosses to be more fun and engaging, but this will always be an optional opt-into hack released in addition to the class rebalance. It will not be pure numbers go up, and in fact, it will include some things like tanking Omega's Evade so that non-!Rapidfire or Stoplock means of hurting it exist.

Here are the general changes from the base game to the hack!

Class-Specific Changes

Knight

Skills

  • !Bladeblitz replaces Equip Armor.

Monk

Attributes

  • Base Magic is now 30 (previously 1).

    • Equipment

    • Monk can now equip Staff weapons and Mage armor.
    • The Kaiser Knuckles now grant Always: Haste in addition to their other effects.

    Skills

    • Level 4 !White replaces HP+10%.
    • Level 4 !Spellblade replaces HP+20%.
    • !Drink replaces Counter. Counter is still Innate.

    General

    • Fists are now Magic Sword OK.

    Thief

    Equipment

    • The Thief Gauntlets now grant +5 speed (previously +3 speed).
    • The Thief Gauntlets now grant Always: Haste. They are still only equippable by Thieves and Freelancers.

    Skills

    • Read Ahead replaces Find Passages. Find Passages is still Innate.
    • Long Reach replaces Sprint. Sprint is still Innate.
    • !Open Fire replaces !Steal. !Steal is still their Job command.
    • !Combine precedes Artful Dodger. Artful Dodger is now the Level 6 ability.
    • !Mug replaces Vigilance. Vigilance is still Innate.

    Drops

    !Combine ammo now drops outside of the Sealed Temple entrance:

    • Buckshot is a guaranteed drop from Black Flames in World 1, Birostris in World 2, and Elm Gigases in World 3.
    • Blastshot is a guaranteed drop from Blood Slimes in World 2 and Executors in World 3.
    • Blitzshot is a guaranteed drop from Mummies in World 3.

    Geomancer

    Attributes

    • Base Strength is now 35 (previously 28).
    • Base Magic is now 52 (previously 48).

    Equipment

    • May now equip Axes and Rods.

    Skills

    • Equip Axes replaces Find Pits. Find Pits is still Innate.
    • Equip Rods replaces Light Step. Light Step is still Innate.

    Dragoon

    Attributes

    • Base Strength is now 48 (previously 42).
    • Base Magic is now 35 (previously 12).

    Equipment

    • Spears, except for Longinus and Dragon Lance, have received significant Attack upgrades.

    Skills

    • Lure replaces Equip Spears.

    Berserker

    Equipment

    • The Rune Axe now has First Strike and its Attack was raised to 102 (previously 71).
    • The Gaia Hammer now has a chance to cast Quake 50% of the time (previously 25%).

    Innate

    • Counter added.
    • Two-Handed added.
    • Exp Up added.

    Skills

    • Exp Up replaces Berserk. Berserk is still Innate.

    Ranger

    Skills

    • !Aim replaces the !Attack command.
    • !Animals replaces !Aim as the Job Command.

    White Mage

    Equipment

    • May now equip Hammers.

    Skills

    • !Revive replaces MP+10%.

    Summmoner

    Skills

    • !Call is moved to the first ability slot, followed by Level 1

    Red Mage

    Design Notes: Red Mage has been redesigned from the ground up. This was intended to fill their niche as a unique hybrid rather than a sub-par fighter and mage past world 2.

    Equipment

    • May now equip Flails and Bows.

    Skills

    • !Dualcast replaces !Red as Job Command. Later, they will have three empty slots with !Dualcast taking place of !Attack but this is unfeasible for the moment.
    • Level 3 !Red replaces Level 1 !Red.
    • !Focus replaces Level 2 !Red. This is to facilitate being able to fight rather than being a pure caster.
    • Level 3 !Time replaces Level 3 !Red.

    Dancer

    Attributes

    • Base Vitality is now 22 (previously 14).

    Innate

    • Magic Shell added.

    Gladiator

    Equipment

    • Cannot equip Spears.

    Oracle

    Equipment

    • May now equip Spears and Knives.

    Monsters

    • The White Serpents in the Wind Shrine now have a chance to drop Daggers.
    • The Octokrakens in the Torna Canal now have a chance to drop Staves.
    • Page 128 now has the Healing Staff as a rare steal.
    • Moldwynd, Calcruthl, Undead Husks, Ra Mages, Kuza Beasts, Skull Eaters, Cure Beasts, Magic Dragons, and Level Checkers now have Elixirs as their rare steal in addition to dropping them normally, opening up varying amounts of crime.
    • Reflect Knights now have Reflect Rings in both drop slots.
    • Metamorph now has Staves of Light in both drop slots.
    • The wild Garula near Carwen and the Ship Graveyard now rarely drops the Thief Knife.

    Equipment

    • The Thief Knife's Attack has been reduced to 18, and is a rare drop for wild Garula. The proc rate for its effect is now 100%. This is to facilitate early criming for parties that lack a Thief.
    • The Mythril Spear's Attack is now 37 (previously 30).
    • The Spear's Attack is now 49 (previously 25). Stolen from Shell Bears.
    • The Javelin's Attack is now 60 (previously 55). Commonly stolen from Bone Dragons; as a result, Sand Bears are common Ethers theft targets.
    • The Trident's Attack is now 48 (previously 38).
    • The Heavy Lance's Attack is now 57 (previously 54).
    • The Rune Chime's Attack is now 70 (previously 45).
    • The Gladius's Attack is now 95 (previously 118), and it is no longer Holy-aspected.
    • The Holy Lance's Attack is now 113 (previously 109).
    • The Longinus's Attack is now 121 (previously 132), and now gives +5 Strength. Theft target for Stingrays.
    • The Blood Sword's Accuracy is now 75% (previously 25%).
    • The Blitz Whip now has a 50% (previously 33%) chance to cast Thundara (previously Thunder).
    • The Gaia Bell's proc rate is now 75% (previously 25%).
    • The Fire Lash's proc rate is now 50% (previously 33%).
